Overwhelmed (lava girl)

With the final project, I played around with selfies and glitched them to find this one piece that looked like my head was split open. I believe we all have that feeling sometimes, whether it's a literally or emotional headache. To me, this seven second animation focuses on the feeling of being overwhelmed and just wanting to release the negative energy to have a fresh start.
